# Frontend Usage

This section explains how to interact with the AURA Protocol frontend application.

## Connecting Your Wallet

To use the AURA Protocol, you need to connect an EVM-compatible wallet (e.g., MetaMask, WalletConnect).
1. Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the header.
2. Select your preferred wallet and follow the prompts to connect.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides a summary of the vault's performance and your position.

### Vault Statistics
- **Total Value Locked (TVL):** The total amount of assets currently managed by the vault.
- **Vault APY:** The Annual Percentage Yield of the vault (currently placeholder, real-time coming soon).
- **Current Vault Strategy:** The active strategy employed by the vault.
- **Total Profit Generated:** The overall profit accumulated by the vault (currently placeholder, real-time coming soon).

### Deposit & Withdraw
This section allows you to deposit stablecoins into the vault and withdraw your vault shares.

#### Deposit
1. Enter the amount of stablecoins you wish to deposit.
2. If this is your first deposit or your allowance is insufficient, you will need to "Approve" the vault to spend your stablecoins.
3. Click "Deposit" to confirm your transaction. You will receive vault tokens (aToken) representing your share.

#### Withdraw
1. Enter the amount of vault tokens (aToken) you wish to withdraw.
2. Click "Withdraw" to receive your stablecoins back.
